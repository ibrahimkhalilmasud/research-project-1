# 📁 Personal Care & Hygiene Products
**Purpose:** Research and product evaluations for bath, body, and hygiene items.

Covers ingredient toxicology assessments, brand comparisons, and safety evaluations
for soaps, detergents, body washes, and hair care products used daily.

**Contains:** BRESIH natural cleaning products, PP405 hair control clinical guide,
Pharmacopia Verbena body wash analysis.

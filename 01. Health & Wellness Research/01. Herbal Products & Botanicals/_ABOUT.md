# 🌿 Herbal Products & Botanicals
**Purpose:** Research hub for all herbal and botanical products evaluated for
health benefits, safety, and sourcing quality.

Each subfolder contains product images, market reports, and risk-benefit briefs
for a specific herb or plant extract.

**Contains:** Ashwagandha, Black Seed Oil, Cordyceps, Haritaki, Shikakai, Shilajit,
Shungite, Spirulina — with market reports and toxicology ratings.

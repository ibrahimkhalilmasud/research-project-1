# 🏊 Swimming Pool Health & Technology
**Purpose:** Evidence-based research on pool sanitation systems and their
health impact — comparing chlorine, saltwater, ozone, and biophilic systems.
**Contains:** Chlorinated pool health assessment (MODERATE risk), saltwater system
report (LOW), ozone systems report (LOW), Hayward Aquarite salt chlorinator review,
biophilic pool health advantages, WYBOT solar robotic pool cleaner research.
